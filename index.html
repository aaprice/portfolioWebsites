About Me

I was born here in Augusta, Georgia in 1984 and have lived here for most of my life. My mother is from New York, and my Father also grew up here. They both instilled in me a passion for learning, technology, and independence. One of the most influential things my father ever told me was, "Never blindly follow, search out the truth for yourself." This has stuck with me throughout many facets of my life.

I graduated from Augusta State University (now Augusta University) in 2010 with a Masters in Public Administration. I spent the next six years in law enforcement, where I eventually worked as a financial crimes investigator. In 2016 I transitioned to a stay at home parent while also working part time as an ESL teacher.

In January 2015, I married my beautiful wife. We met when we both worked for the Sheriff's Office and often like to joke with people that we "met in jail." I have two wonderful step-children through my marriage and one child together with my wife. We all live together in the Evans area.

Projects/Continued Education

I am involved with two side projects/businesses. I co-founded Augusta Cyber Solutions, LLC with my wife Andrea. I am also part of Centaur Security Group as the senior developer. At CSG we have developed a distribution of Ubuntu Linux that has security tools to train the next generation of civilians/soldiers who wnat to learn necessary skills for the IT/Cyber world.

I find value in education, and regularly take new courses through Udemy to increase my knowledge base and skills.
